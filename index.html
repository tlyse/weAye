Welcome

Specializing in the global trade of heavy and large products, the HaFlag Warehouse B2B Marketplace redefines the traditional constraints of the foreign trade industry with digital trade, providing online and offline integrated cross-border transaction and delivery services for furniture and large merchandise.

100% SATISFIED CUSTOMERS

Specialized logistics, warehousing and last-mile delivery services to help streamline fulfillment for suppliers and resellers in the US.

QUALITY SERVICE AFFORDABLE PRICE

Low-rate, fast-track delivery in the Continental US with over 5,000 active suppliers and resellers around the globe and supported by a complete logistics infrastructure, the HaFlag Warehouse B2B Marketplace is a streamlined business solution that gives users the resources they need to seamlessly sell, buy and deliver merchandise
